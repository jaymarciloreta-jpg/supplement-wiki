⏭️ Researched but skip / overhyped

  • Fadogia agrestis — zero human studies; rodent-only T data with testicular/kidney/liver toxicity at ~1 month. Tongkat Ali achieves the same goal with real human evidence.
  • Resveratrol — weak, inconsistent human data, poor bioavailability; some trials show it blunts exercise adaptations.
  • Spermidine — 12-mo SmartAge RCT showed no memory benefit; human longevity case unproven.
  • Fisetin — senolytic hype rests on mouse data; no positive human hard-endpoint trials yet.
  • Inositol — no general cognition/sleep case for a healthy male absent anxiety/PCOS/metabolic indication.
⚠️ Read first. With only two exceptions (Tesamorelin and hCG), nothing here is an FDA-approved drug for the use people seek it for. Most rest on surrogate endpoints (raising GH/IGF-1 or LH/T), not hard outcomes, and most are sold grey-market with no GMP oversight — meaning real risk of contamination, mislabeled content, and dose inaccuracy. WADA bans the large majority. FDA 503A compounding status shifted repeatedly 2023–2026 and several items remain contested — verify before any clinical reliance. Lifestyle & Protocol Notes

🧊 Cold Showers — Cold Shock Proteins

Go cold enough to shiver, then stop shivering — releases cold shock proteins that raise norepinephrine (mood, attention) and act as a strong anti-inflammatory.

  • Most valuable when exercise lasts 3+ hours.
  • For shorter sessions, cold within ~1 hour of lifting can reduce gains — time it carefully.
  • Cold during a rest break between two bouts improved performance on the second bout.

🏋️ Training — Compound Over Isolation

Isolation moves and machines use fewer stabilizers, encourage bad form, and carry higher injury risk for less return.

  • Core lifts: bench, heavy squat, overhead press, rows, deadlifts — barbell over dumbbell for symmetry & stabilization.
  • Lat pulldown is the main worthwhile machine — only if pull-ups aren't yet possible.
  • More routines: onnit.com/academy/fitness

⚡ Testosterone Optimization

  • Deadlifts & wood chopping — most efficient known T-boosting exercises.
  • Cut refined sugars — beneficial effect on T levels.
  • Sleep: each hour contributes ~10% of nightly T production.

📦 Sources & Quality (how to buy well)

Supplements — legal everywhere; quality is the variable. Prefer third-party-tested brands: USP Verified, NSF (NSF Certified for Sport if WADA matters), ConsumerLab, or a batch CoA (identity + potency + heavy-metals/micro).

  • FDA-approved peptides (Tesamorelin, hCG): prescription via a licensed pharmacy.
  • Compounded peptides: only a licensed 503A/503B pharmacy with a prescription, for substances currently permitted. CJC-1295, ipamorelin, BPC-157, TB-500 are not legitimately compoundable as of mid-2026 — verify.
  • Investigational (Retatrutide): clinical trials only until approval.

Grey-market "research peptide" sellers of unapproved injectables are intentionally not listed — mislabeling, contamination, and legal risk. Full detail: Sources and Suppliers note in the vault.
